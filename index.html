Hi! This is Abhinav Jain, currently pursuing my Masters in Robotics from Oregon State University.

Our brain does multitudes of tasks that modern computing isn’t even close to replicating individually, and if we were to, the results would be ineffable.

What motivates me to pursue robotics is just imagining all the possibilities, imagining fifty years down the line how the structure of our very society itself could be altered due to a revolution in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence

My research areas areas broadly include Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Multimodal learning and Embodied Agents
